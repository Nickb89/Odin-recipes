Lasagna

A beautiful finished lasagna

This lasagna will be the talk of the town. It is hearty and fragrant.

Read on to learn how to make this amazing italian triumph.

Ingredients

  • Tomato puree
  • Garlic
  • Lasagne sheets
  • Oregano

Steps:

  1. Brown meat in a pan.
  2. Add garlic and onions.
  3. Add tomato puree.
  4. Add to a dish in layers with pasta sheets.
