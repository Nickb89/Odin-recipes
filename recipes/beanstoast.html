Beans On Toast

A beautiful finished Beans on toast!

This beans on toast will be the talk of the town. It is hearty and fragrant.

Read on to learn how to make this amazing British triumph.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Beans
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Toast bread.
  2. Heat beans.
  3. Combine.
  4. Add grated cheese if you wish.
